Glossary & how to read this ledger
NCB
Nonclassroom-based instruction — charter programs offering less than 80% in-person classroom time (independent study, online/hybrid, resource-center models).
LCFF
Local Control Funding Formula (2013) — California's school funding system, which folded charters into the same funding and planning structure as districts.
LCAP
Local Control and Accountability Plan — the annual goals/spending plan LCFF requires every LEA, including charters, to adopt publicly.
Dashboard
The California School Dashboard — the color-coded (blue/green/yellow/orange/red) accountability system used since 2017 to judge school performance, including charter renewals.
Abuse of discretion
The deferential appeal standard AB 1505 gave the State Board of Education for charter-denial appeals — it must defer to the local authorizer unless that authorizer clearly abused its discretion.
Trailer bill
A bill that implements details of the state budget. Several charter provisions (moratorium extensions, term extensions) have moved this way rather than as standalone legislation.
Verified data
Outside assessment data a charter can offer at renewal (beyond the state Dashboard) to demonstrate achievement gains — a flexibility charter advocates have repeatedly sought to extend.
Chaptered
Once a governor signs a bill, the Secretary of State assigns it a chapter number for that year's session — e.g. "Chapter 486, Statutes of 2019" for AB 1505.

On accuracy: this ledger was assembled from a research conversation, not a direct database pull. Eighteen of the twenty entries have a confirmed bill number, sponsor, and chapter/veto citation drawn from legislative or contemporaneous news sources. Two entries (marked ⚠) reference real vetoes reported in secondary sources but the specific bill number was not independently confirmed — treat those as directional, not citable. Before using any entry in formal work, verify the current statutory text at leginfo.legislature.ca.gov and current implementation guidance at the CDE Charter Schools Division.
